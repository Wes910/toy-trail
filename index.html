How it works

Somewhere out there, a little 3D-printed toy is hiding — tucked into a park bench, a windowsill, or a crack in a wall. Each one has a QR code on it. Scan it, and you'll be able to drop a pin on the map and leave a short note about how finding it made you feel.

Every found toy adds to a growing map of small moments of joy across the city.

Found one already?

Scan the QR code on the toy — it'll take you straight to the page for that toy.
